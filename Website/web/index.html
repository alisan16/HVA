A little bit of History...
The Harvard Vietnamese Association is dedicated to fostering an awareness of Vietnamese and Vietnamese American culture on campus and within the Greater Boston area. Now in our 15th year, we are a vital force on campus and continue to embrace and promote the interests of the Vietnamese and Vietnamese student body at Harvard. read more

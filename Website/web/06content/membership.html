Thank you for your interest in joining HVA. HVA does not have a membership fee. We are a non-profit, self-sustaining, student-run organization, officially recognized by Harvard College.  We operate with the help of generous grants and benefactors. We are open to the entire Harvard University (including those affiliated with Harvard graduate schools and the extension school).

Active  members must attend at least 1 event per semester.  Super Active  members must attend at least 2 events per semester and help to organize at least 1 of these event. Only active members are allowed to vote in board elections. Super Active members have the option of requesting a letter of recommendation from the Board and the faculty adviser at the end of the year.

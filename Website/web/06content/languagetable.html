LANGUAGE TABLE

Meeting Time: 6:00pm every Wednesday
Meeting Location: Quincy Dining Hall  [map]
Contact Point Person: Thanh Nguyen [email]

HVA Language table is an opportunity for interested members to practice their Vietnamese language skills over a casual dinner. Feel free to drop by, whether you are a native speaker or an interested learner. While we try to speak entirely in Vietnamese during this meal, we recognize that members have varying levels of fluency so we will also speak in English. Please do not hesitate to join us because you are unsure of your fluency level. Everyone is welcomed and everyone is here to learn. There is no set schedule. You are not obligated to attend on a weekly basis and you may arrive/leave at your leisure.

HVA uses this opportunity to catch up with one another so come grab a meal with us - even if you're just there to eat.

We hope you'll join us this Wednesday! New members and guests are always welcome.
